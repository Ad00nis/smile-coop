FEATURES

Savings

We help our members cultivate the habit of monthly contribution, which most often are deducted from source (Salaries paid from employment from Smile Communication). This has helped our members built savings, with a view to building up funds for their individual future use.

Loans

Loans are offered to all qualifying members of the Society at the most competitive interest rates compared with other financial institution within the country. Terms of payment usually agreed and made convenient for members and the Society.

Accounting and Reporting

All financial transactions are managed by qualified members of the executive committee, and general and individual savings and loan accounts are set up for all registered members to manage their savings, deposits, withdrawals, loans and other transactions as appropriate. This are easily accessible by members through their unique identifier and credentials.

Privacy, Safety and Security of Members Account.

Our system is backed by multi tiered architecture that provides added security to all transactions done on our platform. Members are entrusted with specific credentials that gives read only access to their individual accounts, excepts for selected members of the executive committee saddled with the responsibilities of managing and updating relevant information. These set of people are given read and write access for account maintenances purpose and the update done are verified quarterly via reconciliation with the available bank statements.

ABOUT US

Smile Communications Staff (VI) Cooperatives Society Limited was incorporated on the 28th day of November, 2017 in Lagos State under the Nigeria Co-operative Societies Act No 98 of 2004. It is a voluntary association of individuals/legal persons united by common bond, who have come together to pursue their socio-economic goals for their own benefits with high regards to non-members.

  • To encourage thrift among members so that each member may cultivate the habit of making savings at least once monthly, with a view to building up funds for their individual future use.
  • To enable members to obtain loans from the society at a minimal interest rate with convenient terms of repayment.
  • To promote mutual co-operation among members.
  • To stock consumer and producer goods for distribution to members and non-members (with priority to members) at fair and reasonable prices.
  • To operate warehouses and cold room facilities for the storage of these goods and let out these facilities to interested parties.
  • To acquire acres of land for sale, lease or Estate development to meet the housing needs of members or for residential, commercial or industrial purposes as a form of investment for the economic benefit of members and other interested parties.
  • To undertake and invest in new ventures beneficial to its members and the Society as approved by the Management Committee. These include Projects Financing; Feasibility Studies, business plan etc.
  • To undertake service businesses that will cater for the primary needs of members and other patrons managed in the overall interest of members and the Society.
  • To encourage fixed deposits from members out of which a fund may be established for giving short-term loans to members, to acquire stock of shares and other rewarding ventures.
  • To raise funds through share holdings by members in the Society to undertake such other activities as are necessary for the attainment of these objects.
  • To invest in Agricultural products. This will include crop and animal farming, buying and selling of Agricultural products and any other agricultural activity.
  • To engage in other economic or social activity as may be approved by the Management Committee or the general meeting of members.
  • To seek loans for residential, commercial or industrial housing development from banks, the co- operative circle and other financial institutions.
  • To secure wholesale mortgage, either for on-lending to members or for housing development and let or sell such dwellings on mortgage basis.
  • To do anything within the law and enter into any transaction which in the Society's opinion is necessary to ensure the proper performance of its functions or the attainment of the above listed objects.

OUR MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

To add value to the lives of members of the Society through the provision of economic, effective and efficient services aimed at assisting members to bridge gaps of difficult and persistent financial deficit through the pool of resources made available by cooperators. Our mission is based and driven by sound management of financial and other resources with integrity and transparency, while making sure that executive management committee and Staff are optimally motivated in order to deliver quality services to the satisfaction of all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be a self-sustaining Society organization that will provide goods and services to its members at a reasonable cost with the aim to encourage them towards productivity, savings, investments and equal distribution of surplus for their general well-being of its members.

Our Services

Thrift Collection

We encourage thrift among members so that each member may cultivate the habit of making savings at least once monthly, with a view to building up funds for their individual future use.

Financial Advisory

We provide professional/Financial Advisory Services - Investment appraisal, feasibility studies, business plans etc.

Short Term Loans

We provide short-term loans for our members at a minimal interest rate with convenient terms of repayment.

Purchase of Home Appliances

Real Estates
